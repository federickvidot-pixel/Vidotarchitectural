Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 4, 2026

Vidot Architectural Furniture Inc. (“we,” “us”) respects your privacy. This policy describes how we handle information when you use our website or get in touch with us.

Information we collect

When you email or call us, we receive what you choose to share (for example your name, phone number, and project details). If you use the contact form on this site, we collect the fields you submit: name, email address, optional phone number, and your message.

How we use it

We use this information only to respond to your inquiry, provide estimates or follow-up about your project, and maintain our business relationship with you. We do not sell your personal information.

Contact form delivery

Messages sent through the website are transmitted using a third-party form service so we can receive email reliably. That service processes your submission on our behalf; do not include sensitive information (such as financial or health details) unless we have agreed to a secure channel.

Retention

We keep correspondence and project-related records only as long as needed for quoting, delivery of our services, and ordinary business and legal requirements.

Your choices

You may ask to access or correct personal information we hold about you, or ask questions about this policy, by contacting us at Vidotarchitectural@bellnet.ca or +1 (514) 739-1433.

Changes

We may update this page from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will change when we do.
