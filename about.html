Our Story

Vidot Architectural Furniture Inc.

Since 1997, Vidot Architectural Furniture Inc. has specialized in crafting custom cabinets and furniture of all kinds. From concept to completion and installation, we ensure that every detail aligns with our customers' requirements, whether for residential or commercial projects.

We work with a wide range of materials to meet your specific needs, and all our creations are crafted in our state-of-the-art 10,000-square-foot workshop in Montreal. Every stage of production is handled in-house by our team of skilled craftsmen, guaranteeing exceptional quality and precision.

From Boutiques to Hotels—Dozens of Projects, Each Built on Excellence and Design.

We deliver high-quality, bespoke solutions tailored to each client's vision. Whether it's a residential kitchen, a commercial space, or a statement piece for a boutique or hotel, we approach every project with the same attention to material, detail, and lasting quality.

Our Team

Founder & master cabinetmaker

Michel Vidot

Michel founded Vidot Architectural Furniture and leads the shop as master cabinetmaker. He brings decades of hands-on experience in bespoke cabinetry and furniture—guiding each project from design intent through fabrication so the finished work matches how you live and work in the space.

Partner

Annie runs day-to-day operations—scheduling, estimates, and follow-up—so clients get clear answers and timelines. She's often who you'll reach first when you call or email the workshop.
