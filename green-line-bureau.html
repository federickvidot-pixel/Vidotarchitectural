Green Line Bureau

An all wood grain pattern with several key repetition thru color and texture. Influenced by the company's line of work, several furniture is built with an emphasis on metal with its surfaces. Blending both wood and metal, that gives a professional and sharp look to its surroundings. Nonetheless, it fills the room with life as the light reflects thru its beautiful coated wood and metal surface.Wood grain and metal surfaces—professional, sharp, and light-filled.
