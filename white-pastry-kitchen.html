The White Pastry Kitchen

An all-white kitchen with stainless steel appliances makes a bold industrial statement. Influenced by its industrial predecessor, this design blends the traditional pastry kitchen look and feel with today's modern home style. The strong, monochromatic palette is punctuated by a dark marble countertop that adds striking contrast and balances the weight of the white—creating a space that feels both professional and inviting.

Sculpted Inspiration

Light bounces off the dark reflective surfaces and bright white cabinets, making the space feel open and invigorating. The dining table, with its dark wood top and turned white legs, extends the same bold contrast into the eating area. Red accents—the stand mixer, toaster, and utensil handles—add energy and warmth, ensuring the kitchen feels both highly functional and genuinely welcoming.
